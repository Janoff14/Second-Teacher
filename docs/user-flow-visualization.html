Week 1 — Score drops
Teacher grades paper, writes score. "Maybe just a bad day."
Week 2 — Another low score
Teacher busy with 5 other classes. Doesn't cross-reference.
Week 3 — Pattern becomes obvious
Teacher finally notices. Tries to remember which topics were weak.
Week 4 — Intervention starts
